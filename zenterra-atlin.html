Overview

Urban Access.
Valley Tranquility.

Atlin is the first phase of Clayton Crest — a brand new community by Zenterra Developments in West Clayton, Surrey. Studio, 1, 2 & 3 bedroom condos designed for connected, modern living.

Featuring a stunning rooftop patio with BBQs and valley views, on-site daycare with resident priority access, fully-equipped fitness centre, co-working space, and a beautifully landscaped community courtyard.

Every suite features 9-foot ceilings, 8-inch wide-plank luxury vinyl flooring, sleek flat-panel cabinetry, quartz countertops, and Samsung stainless steel appliances — in two curated designer colour palettes.

Rooftop Patio + On-Site Daycare
Stunning rooftop with BBQs, fire pits & lounge. On-site daycare with resident first access.
Fitness Centre & Co-Working Space
Fully-equipped gym, lounge, games room, and a co-working patio for remote workdays.
