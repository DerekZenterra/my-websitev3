Our Story

30 Years of Building Better
on Integrity

Since our founding, Zenterra has been guided by a simple belief: homebuyers deserve more. More quality, more care, and greater attention to the details that make a house feel like home from the moment you walk through the door.

We bring a full in-house team to every project — experts in land acquisition, land development, project management, construction, design, marketing, sales, and customer service. That means complete control, complete accountability, and no shortcuts.

What sets Zenterra apart begins long before you step into a showhome. Each project is supported by a fully integrated in-house team, including experts in land acquisition, land development, construction, interior design, marketing, sales, and customer care. With every stage managed internally, we maintain complete oversight of the process from the ground up. This integrated approach ensures a higher level of quality and consistency, because the same team that envisions each home is the one that brings it to life.

Better is our Standard.

We have always held ourselves to a higher standard of excellence. Our philosophy is simple: every homeowner deserves exceptional care and quality at every stage of the journey; from the moment you register your interest to long after you have moved in.

That commitment means the home you move into looks and feels just like the showhome you fell in love with. The finishes are the same. The quality is the same. The pride in the workmanship is the same. Because at Zenterra, “better” isn’t a feature we promote, it’s the standard we deliver every day.

Our Values & Foundations

What We Stand For.
Stand For

Our homes reflect our values. These are the principles that guide every decision we make, on the job site and off it.

01

Get ‘It’ Done

We’re people of action. When we commit to something — a deadline, a detail, a promise to a homebuyer — we follow through. Every time.

02

Pay Attention to Detail

The difference between a good home and a great one lives in the details. We sweat the small stuff so that when you move in, everything feels exactly right.

03

Act with Serious Purpose

Building homes is not something we take lightly. Every decision we make, from land acquisition to the final finishing, is made with intention, care, and a deep respect for the people who will call our homes theirs.

04

Forge Enduring Relationships

We’re not just here for the sale. We build lasting relationships with our homebuyers, our trades, and our communities, because the homes we build are meant to last, and so are the connections we make along the way.

Community Involvement

Building Better Communities in Surrey and Langley — Inside and Out.

At Zenterra, our responsibility extends beyond the homes we build. We take pride in giving back to the communities that have supported us over the years, recognizing that creating a better home also means contributing to a stronger, more vibrant neighbourhood.

◈
Zenterra Cares Food Program
Every day, supporting over 4,000 breakfasts and lunches to students in need across 23 elementary schools and 10 secondary schools in Surrey. Our team regularly volunteers their time to help serve and support the program.
◈
Zenterra Green Program
Partnering with the City of Surrey’s ReLeaf tree-planting initiative to expand and protect Surrey’s urban forest. Every year, Zenterra team members join hundreds of local volunteers to plant trees and shrubs across local parks and schools.
◈
Langley Hockeyhouse & Coastal FC
Sponsoring youth hockey and soccer programs in Langley
◈
Releaf Tree Planting Program
Contributing to reforestation and environmental restoration
◈
