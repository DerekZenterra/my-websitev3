Before You Move In

Step 1 — Pre-Occupancy Walkthrough

A few weeks before your completion date, our Zenterra Homeowner Care team will guide you through a comprehensive pre-occupancy walkthrough of your new construction home. We’ll walk you through your home’s features, appliances, finishings, and systems, and introduce you to your new home warranty coverage so you know exactly what’s protected from day one.

Your confidence matters to us, and we want you moving in feeling fully informed and taken care of.

Mechanical & Systems Review

We walk through your HVAC system, hot water tank, electrical panel, suite entry systems, and all mechanical features — demonstrating how each operates and where controls are located.

Seasonal Maintenance Tips

Download full checklist
🌸

Spring

  • Test smoke and CO detectors; replace batteries
  • Clean or replace HVAC filters
  • Inspect balcony or patio drainage
  • Check window and door seals for winter damage
  • Run water through infrequently used fixtures
  • Clean range hood filter and dryer vent
☀️

Summer

  • Clean window tracks and lubricate sliding doors
  • Inspect caulking around tubs and showers
  • Test all GFI/GFCI outlets
  • Deep clean kitchen exhaust fan grilles
  • Check and clean dishwasher filter
  • Inspect balcony for surface wear or cracking
🍂

Fall

  • Service furnace or heat pump before winter
  • Replace HVAC filters (every 3 months)
  • Test thermostat operation in heat mode
  • Check weatherstripping on exterior doors
  • Flush hot water tank to remove sediment
  • Clean refrigerator coils for efficiency
❄️

Winter

  • Know the location of your suite water shut-off
  • Keep cabinet doors open during extreme cold
  • Report any drafts or condensation to care team
  • Clean humidifier if part of your system
  • Check fire extinguisher charge and accessibility
  • Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans

Flooring Care

Your luxury vinyl plank flooring is highly durable, but a few simple habits will keep it looking new for years. Use felt pads under furniture legs, avoid dragging heavy objects, and clean spills promptly with a damp cloth. For regular cleaning, a dry microfibre mop is ideal. Avoid steam mops and excessive water.

Pro tip: Use a humidifier in winter months to maintain indoor humidity between 35–50%. This protects both your flooring and your home's finishes from the effects of dry air.

/div>
/div>

Kitchen Appliances

Clean your dishwasher filter monthly by removing and rinsing it under warm water. For your refrigerator, vacuum the condenser coils every 6 months to maintain energy efficiency. Your range hood filter should be soaked in hot soapy water monthly if you cook frequently.

Bathroom & Caulking

Inspect caulking around your tub, shower, and sink surrounds twice per year. Surface caulk is designed to be replaced every few years as part of normal home maintenance — this is not a warranty item. Use a bathroom-specific silicone caulk for best results. Run your exhaust fan during and for 15 minutes after every shower to prevent moisture buildup.

Regarding your 2-5-10 warranty: Normal wear and tear, cosmetic items, and maintenance items (like caulking, light bulbs, or filter replacement) are not covered under your New Home Warranty. When in doubt, log into your portal or contact your Customer Care Representative before attempting a fix.

HVAC & Air Quality

Your suite's heating and ventilation system performs best with regular filter replacements. Check your filter every 3 months and replace it when it appears grey or clogged. A clean filter reduces energy consumption, improves air quality, and extends the life of your system.
